About Me

Hi there! I'm Kip Jordan, a passionate Data & Analytics Consultant with over five years of experience in delivering advanced business intelligence solutions. I specialise in data visualisation, Power BI, SQL, and machine learning techniques.

Throughout my career, I've led the evolution from ad-hoc reporting to sophisticated Power BI-driven insights, transforming complex data into actionable insights for key stakeholders. My expertise in orchestrating automated dataflows and building scalable data models has been instrumental in driving strategic decision-making and optimising operational efficiency.

I'm particularly skilled in leveraging Python for custom data analysis and automation, and I have a strong track record of collaborating with cross-functional teams to build and maintain scalable data models. My passion lies in harnessing data to drive innovation and foster growth.

In addition to my technical skills, I hold a Certificate IV in Graphic Design, which enhances my ability to create visually compelling data visualisations and reports. I'm always eager to learn and apply new technologies to solve complex problems.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of the projects I've worked on. I'm excited to connect with like-minded professionals and explore opportunities in the data science field.
